# GrooveX Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 6, 2026  
**Last updated:** July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **GrooveX** (“we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information when you use the GrooveX mobile application for Android (“App”) and related streaming services.

If you have questions, contact us at: **dancepulse.dp@gmail.com**

---

## 1. Overview

GrooveX is a video streaming app for dance, talent, and audition-style content. The App does **not** require you to create a viewer account. You can browse catalogs, watch videos, and save items to a personal “My List” on your device.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information stored on your device (local only)

The App may store the following **on your device only**, using secure on-device storage (Android Keystore–backed secure storage where supported):

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **My List** (video identifiers you save) | Show your saved items inside the App |
| **API server URL** (if you change it in Server settings) | Tell the App which backend to use for videos and metadata |

We do **not** operate a GrooveX user account database for general viewers as part of the App.

### 2.2 Information sent over the network

When you use the App, it communicates with a **content server** (for example, a server URL configured in the App or set by default at build time) to:

- Load video catalogs and metadata (titles, categories, thumbnails)
- Stream video files for playback
- Check that the server is reachable (health check), when applicable

This may include standard technical data your device sends with HTTP/HTTPS requests, such as:

- IP address  
- Device/browser type and operating system version (in request headers)  
- Request timestamps and requested URLs  

That data is processed by **your content server operator** (which may be us or a third party hosting the API). We do not describe here every log your server may keep; server operators should maintain their own privacy practices for server-side logs.

### 2.3 Optional in-app “Server settings” login

The App includes an optional **Server settings** screen protected by a simple username and password. This gate is used **only inside the App** to allow changing the API base URL. Credentials are checked on the device against values defined in the App; they are **not** used to create a cloud GrooveX account for viewers.

### 2.4 Information we do not collect through the App

As of this policy, the App does **not** intentionally collect:

- Name, email, or phone number for viewer accounts  
- Precise location (GPS)  
- Contacts, photos, or files from your device (except media you choose to upload via separate **web admin** tools, not through the consumer App)  
- Advertising identifiers for targeted ads (the App does not show third-party ads)

We do **not** sell your personal information.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services and Content

### 3.1 Your streaming backend

Video and catalog data are served from the API URL configured for the App. Whoever operates that server (including you, if self-hosted) is responsible for the content and for any server-side logging or storage.

### 3.2 Thumbnails and sample media

Catalog thumbnails or demo streams may be loaded from:

- The same API server, or  
- Public CDNs (for example, image or sample video hosts used for demonstration content)

Those providers receive normal network request data when your device loads those assets. Refer to their privacy policies for details.

### 3.3 Expo / app distribution

The App is built with [Expo](https://expo.dev) and distributed via Google Play. Google’s policies and privacy practices apply to downloads, updates, and Play Store account data. See [Google Play’s Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## 4. How We Use Information

We use information described above to:

- Provide streaming, browsing, search, and “My List” features  
- Maintain App configuration (API URL) you choose  
- Improve reliability and fix errors  

We do not use viewer data for targeted advertising in the App.

---

## 5. Data Retention

- **My List** and **API URL** on device: kept until you clear App data, uninstall the App, or remove items from My List.  
- **Server-side logs** (if any): retained according to the server operator’s policies.

---

## 6. Security

We use reasonable measures appropriate to an app of this type, including secure on-device storage for local preferences. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you use **HTTP** (non-encrypted) to reach a server, data may be visible on the network path; we recommend **HTTPS** for production deployments.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

GrooveX is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through the App. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us at **dancepulse.dp@gmail.com** and we will take appropriate steps.

---

## 8. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information. For data stored **only on your device**, you can:

- Remove items from **My List** in the App  
- Clear the App’s storage in Android **Settings → Apps → GrooveX → Storage → Clear data**  
- Uninstall the App  

For data held on a **backend server**, contact the server operator or us at **dancepulse.dp@gmail.com**.

## 9. International Users
